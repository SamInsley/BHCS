About the Show

The Broadway Horticultural and Craft Show is an annual event that takes place on the village green in Broadway on the second Saturday in September. Locals and anyone wishing to enter can share with everyone what they've been able to grow or create and then have it judged against others by experts. It is run every year by a committee of volunteers with the current team pictured below.
