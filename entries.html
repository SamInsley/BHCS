Enter the 2026 Show

Here you can find an online entry form for the 2026 Broadway Horticultural and Craft Show. All adult classes are 30 pence to enter and child classes are 10 pence (apart from E5 which is £5). You will be expected to pay this in full (in cash) when bringing in your entries on the morning of the show (this includes any entries you submitted but did not fulfil). All categories are single entry with the exception of Section H which you may enter multiple times, to do this just click on the entry button as many times as you require. Before filling in this entry form please make sure you have read the schedule and you understand the rules and restrictions associated with each class (such as size restrictions) as these are not present on the entry form. You should receive an email shortly after submitting confirming we have received your entry, including all categories entered. This entry form will close at 12pm on Saturday 5th of September. If you have any queries about your entry, need to amend or confirm what you have entered then please contact us as soon as possible to ensure we can get this ready for the show.
